Who Am I?

I'm Nia Clark and I live in Atlanta, GA. I'm a self-identified Blerd with a variety of passions: anime, literature, film, theatre, music...pretty much arts in general because they make the world beautiful. I spent nearly a decade in warehouse work, mainly machine operation, and heavily considered welding before applying to App Academy.
